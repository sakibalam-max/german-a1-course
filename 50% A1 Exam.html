About This Course

Our German A1 course is designed to provide you with a solid foundation in the German language. We cover essential grammar, vocabulary, and common phrases needed for everyday communication. Whether you're planning to travel, work, or simply learn a new language, this course will equip you with the necessary skills to confidently navigate basic German conversations.

Key topics include:

  • Greetings and Introductions
  • Numbers and Counting
  • Basic Grammar (Articles, Nouns, Verbs)
  • Daily Routines and Activities
  • Ordering Food and Drinks
  • Asking for Directions
